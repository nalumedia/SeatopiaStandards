Why Seatopia: The Science

Bioavailability, ratios, and competitive advantage

Why Whole Fish > Supplements

Omega-3 in whole fish exists in phospholipid and triglyceride forms, which are 2–3× more bioavailable than the ethyl ester form used in standard fish oil capsules. A single serving of Ora King King Salmon (1,606 mg EPA+DHA) is equivalent to 16–24 high-quality capsules by bioavailable absorption, not just by mg content.

Citation: Dyerberg et al. (2010). Prostaglandins, Leukotrienes and Essential Fatty Acids.

Omega-6:Omega-3 Ratio

Western diets average 15:1 to 20:1 omega-6 to omega-3 — promoting inflammation. The ideal ratio is 1:1 to 4:1. All Seatopia products tested show ratios under 1.1:1 — meaning more omega-3 than omega-6 on a per-gram basis.
